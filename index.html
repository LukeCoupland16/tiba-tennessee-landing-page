01

Lakeside Suites

45 keys, lake views, in-room rituals

A 45-key retreat shaped from the land: a lake revealed from a spring, with suites individually oriented to the water — some at the bank, others elevated for horizon-line views. Architecture grown from the site, not placed on it. Balinese craft meets Tennessee forest — teak, stone, and native geology forming spaces where indoor-outdoor living is not a feature, but the foundation.

Private decks & jacuzzis — each suite opens directly onto the water's edge
In-room rituals — curated wellness experiences woven into every stay
ADR uplift — premium positioning with upsell packages driving revenue per key
Comp set: $595–$2,100+/night — positioned alongside Blackberry Mountain & Boltfarm
02

The Lobby

Fire-cooking, ritual dining, gathering

The heart of the retreat — where ritual meets craft, and every meal becomes ceremony. Nourishing, ritual-led dining featuring lakeside breakfasts, fireside dinners, chef events, and curated picnics. Culinary programming by Tavolo Group, the creative force behind iconic Bali venues, bringing a sharp focus on guest experience and operational excellence to Tennessee.

Restaurant, bar & private dining — multiple F&B revenue streams from a single operation
Tastings & curated picnics — high-margin experiential upsells
Full-property buyouts — ideal scale for boutique gatherings and high-yield groups
Event F&B — retreat partnerships and private event catering as diversified revenue
03

Nature & Wellness

Sound healing, contrast therapy, trails

Not just a spa, but a connection to the land. Wellness as architecture, not amenity. Ritual and stillness shape the stay — where "silence is the main amenity." Walking distance from Foster Falls, guests gain effortless access to a 60-foot waterfall, canyon overlooks, and miles of protected hiking trails — an amenity no hotel can build, but one that defines the Tiba experience.

Sound healing & meditation — therapeutic programming in nature-integrated spaces
Ice-baths & contrast therapy — high-demand wellness offerings with premium pricing
Foster Falls access — 60-ft waterfall, canyon overlooks, and protected trails at your doorstep
Corporate wellness & retreats — paid classes, private bookings, and group packages
